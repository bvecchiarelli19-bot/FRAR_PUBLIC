Macro Regime:
Context for Your Capital

FRAR tells you how much risk to take. The Macro Regime tells you where to take it.

We classify the economic environment using the rate of change in growth and inflation‚Äînot absolute levels. This creates four distinct phases, each favoring different sectors:

  • ‚óè Goldilocks ‚Äî Growth accelerating, inflation falling. Favors: Tech, growth stocks, long-duration assets.
  • ‚óè Reflation ‚Äî Growth accelerating, inflation rising. Favors: Cyclicals, commodities, financials.
  • ‚óè Stagflation ‚Äî Growth decelerating, inflation rising. Favors: Energy, defensives, cash. Avoid bonds.
  • ‚óè Deflation ‚Äî Growth decelerating, inflation falling. Favors: Treasuries, utilities, quality.

Signals derived from FRED economic data (industrial production, payrolls, retail sales, CPI, PCE).

FAQ

Common questions

Is this market timing?

No. Market timing tries to predict where prices will go. FRAR measures current market structure‚Äîhow volatile, how fat-tailed, how trendy. It's risk management, not prediction. We're not saying "the market will crash," we're saying "the market is currently fragile."

How is this different from just watching the VIX?

VIX is one input. FRAR combines three orthogonal measures‚Äîvolatility clustering, tail risk (Hill estimator), and market memory (Hurst exponent). The combination catches regime changes that single metrics miss, with fewer false signals.

What if FRAR is wrong?

It will be‚Äîsometimes. FRAR underperforms in low-volatility melt-ups and can't react to sudden shocks between monthly signals. It's designed to reduce average drawdowns, not eliminate all losses. We publish known failure modes for full transparency.

Why is the basic signal free?

The monthly regime signal (LOW/MID/HIGH/CRISIS) will always be free. That's the core insight, and we want it accessible to everyone. Premium features‚Äîsector-level allocations, the macro regime overlay, weekly signals, email alerts, and API access‚Äîare where the business model lives. Free users get the "what." Paid users get the "how" and "why."

Can I verify the backtest myself?

Yes. The methodology is fully transparent and uses publicly available data (SPY, sector ETFs). Pro subscribers get access to the full research dashboard with complete historical data and code.
